Loveyatri (transl.?Love travellers; Hindi pronunciation: [l??ja?t???]) is a 2018 Bollywood romantic drama film produced by Salman Khan under Salman Khan Films and directed by debutante Abhiraj Minawala. It is inspired from the Telugu film Devadasu. It stars Aayush Sharma and Warina Hussain in its lead roles, and was released on 5 October 2018.
