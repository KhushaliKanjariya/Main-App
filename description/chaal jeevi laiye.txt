Chaal Jeevi Laiye! (transl.?Come, let's live!) is a 2019 Indian Gujarati-language comedy-drama road film written and directed by Vipul Mehta. Produced by Rashmin Majithia, the film stars Siddharth Randeria, Yash Soni and Aarohi Patel.[2] The soundtrack was composed by Sachin–Jigar.[3] It is the highest grossing Gujarati film of all time.
